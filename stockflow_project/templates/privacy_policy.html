Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2025

At StockFlow, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our inventory management application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up for StockFlow, we collect your username, email address, and password.
  • Inventory Data: Information about your products, transactions, and categories that you input into the application.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data about how you interact with our application, such as IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the StockFlow application.
  • Improve user experience and develop new features.
  • Communicate with you, including sending updates or notifications.
  • Ensure the security of our application and prevent fraud.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • With your consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of StockFlow, our users, or the public.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of receiving non-essential communications.
  • Contact us to request more information about how your data is handled.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@stockflow.com.
